What We Do

VP & Associates, Inc. provides a full range of structural engineering, steel detailing services and inspection services. With over 30 years of experience, our engineers and steel detailers have knowledge and demonstrated mastery in all facets of structural engineering and steel detailing including, but not limited to, developing plans and specifications for construction of new and existing buildings/tanks, as well as providing inspection services.

We provide services to corporate industrial contractors, commercial architects, and steel fabricators utilizing the most current 3-D computer analysis systems available, ACAD drafting, SDS2 steel detailing, and coordinating available research materials. Our engineers and detailers have initiated, engineered, and completed countless multi-faceted design and construction projects which have met goals to reduce construction cost and time.

We sign and seal shop drawings including miscellaneous metals. Within the last two and a half years we have designed connections for more than 7000 tons of steel with structures reaching over 300 ft. in height. The connection designs involved all levels of complexities from braced frame to simple shear and moment connections.

As designers of building envelopes, VP & Associates, Inc. understands the design intent of the structure. Therefore, we communicate with the Engineer of Record to resolve any loading conditions that may adversely affect the main structure, its members, and/or connections.

Our communication to the Engineer of Record and to the detailer is concise and expedient. Once we've completed the connection design, we will send the detailer sketches depicting all relevant information for detailing. We use 3-D and in-house software to create the calculation which we will sign, seal, and submit to the Engineer of Record. If the detailing is done in SDS2 by other detailers, we can accept an SDS2 model.

Once we've received the SDS2 model from the detailer via e-mail, we will complete the connection design in SDS2 and e-mail the detailer the completed connection design and model. The detailer is not required to model the connection. The calculation is then extracted from SDS2 and printed out by VP & Associates, Inc. We will then send the signed and sealed calculation to the fabricator.

VP & Associates, Inc. has the capability of generating 3-D model structural analysis which would design and size the individual members for over-all building enveloper. The same model could easily be imported directly into SDS2 for detailing. This would cut down the detailer modeling time and expedite the detailing process.

Currently, we are licensed in the states of Florida, Kentucky, Maryland, Michigan, Pennsylvania, Tennessee, and Virginia and can obtain licensure in other states.
